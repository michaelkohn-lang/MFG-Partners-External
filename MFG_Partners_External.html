Investment Thesis
Picks & shovels for the sports gold rush.
As institutional capital floods into team ownership and league equity, the underlying service infrastructure must professionalize. MFG Partners provides the operating tools to drive revenue and reduce costs.

Contracted Revenue

Long-term agreements with professional teams and leagues provide predictable, recurring cash flows that de-risk the portfolio and support consistent distributions.

Asset-Light Model

Commission-based and fee-for-service models deliver high margins with minimal capital reinvestment — maximizing free cash flow generation across every vertical.

Cross-Sell Flywheel

Each operating company drives referrals across the platform, compounding client value over time. One team relationship unlocks four distinct revenue streams.

Sector Tailwinds

The middle market of sports services is fragmented and consolidating, favoring disciplined acquirers with operational expertise and institutional relationships.
